McPartland Publications

The references below link to recent publications, manuscripts under editorial consideration, and manuscripts in preparation. These documents are provided in support of Dr. McPartland’s K23 application and are intended for perusal by NIH personnel. As most of these works are not yet final, please do not distribute.
